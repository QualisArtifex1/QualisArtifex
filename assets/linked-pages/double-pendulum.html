Mini Lesson

Key point: this pendulum follows precise laws, but long-term prediction becomes difficult because small measurement errors grow.

chaos ≠ randomness
chaos = deterministic rules + sensitive initial conditions

Classroom move: set the ghost offset to 0.005°, pause, drag a bob, then press play. Ask: “Would measuring better solve the prediction problem forever?”
