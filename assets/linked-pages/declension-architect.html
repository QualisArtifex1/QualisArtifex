How to Play

Goal: place the one current ending into the correct chart cell.

  • One ending at a time: the draw pile shows only the current randomized ending.
  • Drag or tap: drag the tile, or simply tap the chart cell where it belongs.
  • Check Chart: correct cells lock. Wrong cells disappear and return to the randomized draw pile.
  • Hint: reveals one empty cell and counts as one error.

The * tile marks places where the ending is stem-dependent rather than one predictable form.
